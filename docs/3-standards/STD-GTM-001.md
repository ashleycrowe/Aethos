## 🎯 POSITIONING

**Category:** Enterprise Intelligence Layer for Microsoft 365  
**Tagline:** "The Anti-Intranet: Organizational Clarity Through Intelligence"

**Value Proposition:**
> "Transform your Microsoft 365 tenant from a chaotic file graveyard into an intelligent operational layer. Aethos identifies waste, connects siloed data, and delivers clarity—without reading your files."

**Key Differentiators:**
1. **Metadata-First:** Rich intelligence without content reading (Base tier)
2. **Identity-First Data Model:** Track people, not just files
3. **Universal Adapter:** M365 + Slack + Google + Box
4. **Instant ROI:** 30-day payback on storage waste recovery

---

## 💰 PRICING STRATEGY

### Tiered Model (Per Tenant/Month)

**14-Day Trial: $0**
- 1 workspace maximum
- 100 assets limit
- View-only mode
- No credit card required

**Base Tier: $499/tenant/month**
- Unlimited workspaces
- Metadata enrichment & intelligence scoring
- Tag-based workspace auto-sync
- Cross-provider search (metadata-only)
- Universal Adapter Pattern (Tier 1 & 2)
- Governance dashboard
- Identity reconciliation (metadata-only)

**AI+ Tier: $698/tenant/month**
- All Base features +
- AI content reading & analysis
- Semantic/vector search
- Document summarization
- Smart duplicate detection (content-level)
- Predictive workspace suggestions

**Pricing Philosophy:**
- No per-user pricing (tenant-level)
- Transparent, predictable costs
- Clear feature differentiation
- AI+ as premium upsell (+$199/mo)
