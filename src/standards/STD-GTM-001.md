## 💰 PART 1: PRICING ARCHITECTURE (SMART ROI)

### 1.1 Commercial Tiers
| Tier | Product | Price | Primary Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1 (The Audit)** | The Flashlight | $2,500 (One-time) | Ghost Town Report & Storage Waste Audit. |
| **Tier 2 (The Platform)** | The Nexus | $6.00 / user / mo | Continuous Governance & Workspace Builder. |
| **Tier 3 (The Pulse)** | The Pulse | $10.00 / user / mo | Social Engagement, Multi-channel Comms & Mobile Push. |

### 1.2 Specialized Verticals
- **Education (EDU):** Flat Site License based on Faculty/Staff count. Students included (Read-only).
- **Government (GCC):** Per-user premium (+20%) for Azure Gov and US-based support.

### 1.3 Add-on Features
- **Mobile Push Notifications**: Included in Tier 3 or +$1.50/user/mo for Tier 2.
- **Dedicated Persistence Layer**: (Decision Point) For clients requiring local data residency, a dedicated Supabase instance can be provisioned for +$500/mo.

## 🚀 PART 2: CAMPAIGN ORCHESTRATION

### Phase 1: The "Operational Clarity" Campaign (Months 1-3)
- **Target:** IT Directors & CISOs.
- **Narrative:** "Operational Intelligence: Illuminating the Hidden Costs of M365."
- **Focus:** External Exposure (Risk/Leaked IP) and Version Bloat (Waste/Cost).
- **Goal:** Drive Tier 1 Audits to prove immediate ROI through "Digital Hygiene."

### Phase 2: The "Search" Campaign (Months 4-6)
- **Target:** Internal Comms & Ops.
- **Narrative:** "Stop searching. Start finding."
- **Goal:** Convert Tier 1 clients into long-term Tier 2 Platform users.

### Phase 3: The "Pulse" Campaign (Months 7-9)
- **Target:** CHROs & Internal Comms.
- **Narrative:** "Operational Soul: Transforming Efficiency into Engagement."
- **Goal:** Upsell existing Tier 2 users to Tier 3 for cultural alignment.
